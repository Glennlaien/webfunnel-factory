# WallFit Example Product Brief

Women-focused Wall Pilates subscription product.

Users enter a mobile-first Web2App onboarding funnel, answer a personalization quiz, receive a plan summary, see a plan-ready bridge, then reach a subscription paywall.

## Product Direction

- Category: Wall Pilates / at-home fitness subscription
- Audience: Women aged 25-55
- Core promise: Personalized at-home Wall Pilates plan for weight loss, toning, posture, and habit building
- Funnel type: Direct-response fitness quiz app
- Visual direction: Bright, app-like, rose-pink CTA, clean white option cards, trustworthy but conversion-focused

## Avoid

- Generic wellness magazine look
- Traditional landing page
- Dark gym aesthetic
- Overly luxury beige/sage visual system
- Hard-filled page count with unnecessary questions
