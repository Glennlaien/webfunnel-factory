# paywall_page

The paywall page presents subscription plans and converts the user.

## Required Content

- Headline
- Subheadline
- Plan cards
- Recommended plan
- CTA
- Trust or guarantee line
- Terms or billing disclaimer

## Design Expectations

- Clear hierarchy
- Recommended plan visually obvious
- Direct-response enough to convert
- Trustworthy enough for paid subscription

## Avoid

- Hiding prices
- Too many plan choices
- Generic SaaS pricing table styling
- Paywall disconnected from the quiz result
